About LeetEnglish

Hi, Welcome to leetEnglish, LeetEnglish is an app that allows users to increase their vocabulary level by reading. In this app, you will be able to read articles shared by another user as public articles, you will be able to read through the entire content and get definitions as well as add an image to help your memorization of the vocabulary. Besides that, you will be able to import your own article into the app, and you also able to implement the whole feature as public articles, get definitions, memorization vocabulary with images, and getting practice with the vocabulary.

Most importantly, In leetEnglish you will be able to connect with another user, adding your own tweets, share your moment with another user!, let diving in!

In this portion, you will able to access two kind of articles, Public Articles and Your Own Article. Public article means the articles share by other user, you own article will be the article you import.

Public Articles

  1. In this sub-portion you will see all the category users has share, so far you can only see four portion, business, entertainment, health and technology.

  2. Click on one of the category you will able to read all the article

  3. after your click on the article you will see the read board section

  4. as you reading if you encounter any vocabulary that your don't recognize, you just copy and paste to get the definition and add image to memorization

  5. you will be adding a image to memorization that vocabulary

Import Own Articles

  1. In this sub section your will able to import you own article to read.

  2. you can create you own category for the article you create add article
